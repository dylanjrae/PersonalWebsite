Tire Temperature & Pressure Monitoring System

I led a team of 7 electrical engineering students to develop a system that could measure tire temperature and pressure from within the a tire. The data is transmitted wirelessly to a hub for later analysis. This project was completed in coordination with the University of Calgary FSAE Schulich Formula Racing Team and an electrical engineering design class I was taking at the time.

Quick Specs

  • Software: Java, C++
  • Sensors: 3 IR temperature sensors and 1 pressure sensor
  • Transceiver: NRF24L01
  • Microcontroller: ATMEGA328P
