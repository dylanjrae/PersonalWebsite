About Me

I am a student at the University of Calgary pursuing degrees in electrical engineering and finance graduating April 2022. Currently, I am working part-time as a FinTech research assistant at the Haskayne School of Business where I work with various blockchain technologies. Last year I interned for 14-months as a technical business analyst at EECOL Electric assisting the executive team develop and deploy a nation-wide growth strategy.

Previous experience includes co-founding an equine wearables startup and field-engineering work in the oil and gas industry.

Passionate about how we can bridge engineering and business to solve some of the world's most important problems.
